July 16th, 1998

Homepage: Sega's "Sonic" title for Dreamcast

is revealed on the company's website!

Sega Enterprises' first pure-bred title for Dreamcast is "Sonic Adventure", the company announced on its website.

The release of Sega's Dreamcast title "GODZILLA" (tentative) has already been announced for the end of this year, and it is being developed by General Entertainment. "Sonic Adventure" will be developed by Sonic Team, just like the Mega Drive Sonic series, and will be the first Dreamcast title to be developed and marketed by Sega.

Currently, the Sega website confirms that there will be two presentations (free admission) for general users at the Tokyo International Forum on August 22 (from 10:00 and 13:30). This is exactly the same format as Warp's presentation of "D no Shokutaku 2" in May. At the time of Warp's presentation, there was a problem that some fans could not enter the International Forum due to the popularity of the event, but I expect that Sega, which must have witnessed such a problem, is planning some countermeasures.

Details of the game content have not been disclosed, but we will introduce further information as soon as it becomes available.
